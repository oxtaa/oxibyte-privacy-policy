oxibyte Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6/24/23

The privacy policy of oxibyte ensures that no user, server, or message information is collected or stored. Only the information provided by the user is used to execute the bot's commands and is completely erased from the bot's database once the action is completed. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it at all times.
